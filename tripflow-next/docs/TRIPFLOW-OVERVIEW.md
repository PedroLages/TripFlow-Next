# TripFlow AI - App Overview

## What is TripFlow AI?

TripFlow AI is a travel planning application that helps users plan, organize, and manage their trips from start to finish. It combines the power of artificial intelligence with full manual control, allowing travelers to either generate complete itineraries instantly or build every detail themselves.

The app was inspired by real-world travel planning challenges experienced during a 22-night Asia trip across 6 cities. The founder's experience revealed major pain points: managing multiple documents, manual data entry across different files, difficulty collaborating with travel companions, complex multi-currency budget tracking, and no centralized way to track bookings and deadlines.

TripFlow AI solves these problems by providing a single, integrated platform for all aspects of travel planning.

---

## What Does TripFlow AI Do?

TripFlow AI guides travelers through every stage of their journey:

### Before the Trip
- **Discover** destinations and activities based on personal interests
- **Plan** detailed day-by-day itineraries with hourly schedules
- **Budget** with real-time currency conversion and spending forecasts
- **Book** flights, hotels, and activities while tracking confirmation details
- **Collaborate** with travel companions in real-time

### During the Trip
- **Navigate** with interactive maps showing all activity locations
- **Track** expenses as they happen and monitor remaining budget
- **Access** booking confirmations and reference numbers instantly
- **Adjust** plans based on weather, crowds, or changing preferences

### After the Trip
- **Review** actual spending versus original budget
- **Preserve** itineraries for future reference or to share with others
- **Learn** from travel patterns to improve future trips

## Core Features

### 1. AI Trip Generator (Optional)

Users can generate complete trip itineraries by providing simple inputs:
- Destination and travel dates
- Budget range (budget, mid-range, or comfortable)
- Number of travelers
- Interests and travel style (foodie, adventure, culture, relaxation, etc.)
- Any specific requirements or constraints

The AI generates a day-by-day itinerary with:
- Scheduled activities with time blocks
- Restaurant recommendations for each meal
- Transportation options between locations
- Estimated costs for each activity
- Local tips and cultural context

**Key Point**: Users have full control. They can use AI as a starting point and then edit everything manually, skip AI entirely and build from scratch, or use AI selectively for just recommendations.

### 2. Interactive Itinerary Builder

A visual timeline where users can:
- Drag and drop activities between days
- Set start and end times for each activity
- See conflicts when activities overlap
- Add notes and descriptions
- Mark activities as booked, tentative, or completed
- Pin activities to specific dates and times

The builder shows a daily view with hourly slots, making it easy to plan realistic days without over-scheduling.

### 3. Multi-Currency Budget Tracker

Travelers often deal with multiple currencies on a single trip. TripFlow AI handles this automatically:

- **Real-time conversion** between currencies at current exchange rates
- **Category breakdowns** (flights, accommodation, activities, food, transport, shopping)
- **Budget vs actual spending** with visual progress indicators
- **Three budget tiers** (budget, mid-range, comfortable) for trip planning
- **Spending alerts** when approaching budget limits
- **Daily budget views** to track spending throughout the trip

Users can set a total budget, allocate amounts to each category, and track every expense during travel.

### 4. Booking Tracker

Never lose a confirmation or miss a booking deadline again:

- **Track what's booked** versus what's still estimated
- **Store booking references** and confirmation links
- **Deadline reminders** for when to book (e.g., "book hotel 3 months in advance")
- **Priority color coding** to highlight urgent bookings
- **Upload confirmations** as PDFs or images
- **Quick access** during travel to show tickets and reservations

The booking tracker draws from travel research to suggest optimal booking timelines for different types of reservations.

### 5. Activity & Restaurant Recommendations

Discover things to do and places to eat:

- **AI-powered suggestions** based on location, time of day, and user preferences
- **Curated database** of hidden gems and local favorites
- **Save favorites** for future trips
- **Ratings and reviews** from other travelers
- **Filter by** price range, distance, category, and more
- **Add to itinerary** with one tap

Recommendations include both popular attractions and off-the-beaten-path experiences that locals love.

### 6. Collaborative Planning

Travel with others? Plan together without the back-and-forth:

- **Invite collaborators** via email or shareable link
- **Real-time sync** - changes appear instantly for everyone
- **Permission levels** - Owner, Editor, or Viewer access
- **Comments and discussions** on specific activities
- **Voting** to decide on activities democratically
- **Activity suggestions** that collaborators can add

Collaborators don't even need an account initially - guests can view and comment through a shared link.

### 7. Expense Splitting

Split costs fairly with travel companions:

- **Multiple split types**: Equal split, custom amounts, percentages, or itemized
- **"Who paid" tracking** - record who actually paid for each expense
- **Settlement calculator** - instantly see who owes whom and how much
- **Multi-currency support** - split expenses even when paid in different currencies
- **Export summaries** for easy settlements after the trip

The app minimizes the number of transactions needed to settle up, so friends can settle debts efficiently.

## What Makes TripFlow AI Different?

### Hybrid AI + Manual Approach
Most apps either are fully AI (little control) or fully manual (no assistance). TripFlow AI lets users choose - use AI as a co-pilot or a full autopilot, with the option to take manual control at any point.

### Built from Real Travel Experience
The app was designed by someone who planned a complex 6-city, 22-night Asia trip. Every feature addresses a real problem encountered during that process.

### End-to-End Coverage
From inspiration to execution to post-trip review, TripFlow AI covers the entire travel journey - not just one aspect like booking or itinerary display.

### Collaboration First
Travel is social. The app is designed from the ground up for groups, with real-time collaboration, expense splitting, and shared decision-making built into the core experience.
