# Travel Planning App - Features Specification

## Trip Overview Dashboard

- Trip summary with customizable duration, destinations, and date range
- Destination overview cards with nights, dates, and highlights
- Quick access to daily itinerary, budget, and each destination
- Countdown timer to departure
- Multi-stop trip visualization with route map

## Day-by-Day Itinerary View

- Daily schedules with timed activities (Time | Activity | Transport | Cost | Booking Status)
- Tap-through to detailed activity information
- Checkboxes to mark activities complete
- Notes/journaling per day
- Weather widget for each day/location
- Drag-and-drop activity reordering
- Time conflict detection

## Budget Tracker

- Customizable budget tiers (Budget | Mid-Range | Comfortable | Custom)
- Booking progress tracker (estimated vs. actual costs)
- Category breakdown with progress bars
- Expense logging during trip with receipt photo capture
- Multi-currency converter with real-time rates
- Visual charts for spending by category
- Per-destination budget allocation
- Spending alerts and budget warnings

## Destination Research Modules

Each destination section should include:

### Transport
- Public transit system info (lines, fares, hours)
- Taxi/ridesharing guides (Uber, Lyft, local alternatives)
- Airport transfer options
- Inter-city transport connections (flights, trains, buses)
- Car rental information
- Bike/scooter sharing options

### Attractions
- Must-see spots with costs, hours, best times
- Ticket booking requirements and advance purchase warnings
- Tips for avoiding crowds
- Photo spot recommendations
- Free/low-cost alternatives
- Accessibility information

### Food
- Restaurant recommendations by price tier
- Must-try dishes and local specialties
- Hidden gems & local favorites
- Food areas/markets
- Dining etiquette and tipping customs
- Dietary accommodation options (vegan, gluten-free, etc.)

### Practical Tips
- Weather by travel dates with seasonal packing advice
- Local payment methods and cash vs. card acceptance
- Cultural etiquette and customs
- Emergency information and local contacts
- Time zone and clock change information
- Safety advisories

## Interactive Maps & Routes

- Walking routes for each day with distance/time estimates
- Public transit navigation guides
- Transit directions to attractions
- Offline map support with downloadable areas
- Route optimization for multiple stops
- Points of interest layer
- Real-time location sharing with travel companions
