### Double Bed vs Twin
- **Japan:** Many hotels only offer twin beds. When booking, specifically request "double bed" or confirm room type.
- **China/Hong Kong:** Double beds more common but confirm when booking.
- **What to look for:** "Double bed," "Queen bed," "One large bed"

### Serviced Apartment Value (Tokyo)
For 6 nights in Tokyo, a serviced apartment split two ways is excellent value:
- Kitchen → cook breakfast, save $10-15/day × 6 = $60-90 saved
- Washer → no laundromat hassle
- Living space → spread out after 2 weeks of travel
- Per-night cost similar to mid-range hotel when split

## CRITICAL BOOKING TIPS

1. **Free Cancellation is Non-Negotiable** (Typhoon Season)
   - Shanghai, Hong Kong, Osaka, Kyoto, Tokyo: Book free cancellation
   - Beijing: Less critical (weather is good in September)

2. **Book 3-4 Months Ahead** (April-May 2026)
   - Lock in good rates with free cancellation
   - Especially for: ryokans, popular hotels (Dormy Inns book early)

3. **Re-Check 6-8 Weeks Before** (July 2026)
   - If prices dropped, rebook and cancel original
   - Ryokans often have strict cancellation policies

4. **Confirm These When Booking:**
   - Double bed (not twin)
   - Free cancellation terms
   - Check-in/check-out times
   - 24-hour front desk (Hong Kong especially)
   - Foreign guest accepted (China especially)

5. **Payment Methods:**
   - China: Some hotels want cash deposit or Alipay/WeChat
   - Hong Kong/Japan: Credit cards widely accepted
