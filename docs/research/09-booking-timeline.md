## Cancellation Strategy

**Golden Rule:** Always book free cancellation when possible.

**Why pay 5-15% more?**
- Typhoon season (Aug-Sep) = unpredictability
- Prices fluctuate
- Plans change
- Peace of mind

**Strategy:**
1. **Book free cancellation** now (Phase 1)
2. **Monitor prices** every 2-4 weeks
3. **If prices drop:** Rebook at lower rate, cancel original
4. **If prices stable:** Keep existing booking
5. **2-4 weeks before:** Consider locking in non-refundable if confident (but free cancellation still safer)

**Exceptions to free-cancellation rule:**
- Ryokans (often have 7-14 day cancellation windows â€” accept this for the experience)
- Some budget airlines (non-refundable by default)
- Forbidden City tickets (non-refundable but mandatory)
