## 10. Money-Saving Tips

1. **Book flights early** -- 6-8 weeks ahead saves $100-300 on inter-city flights
2. **Skip the JR Pass** -- individual tickets are cheaper for this specific route (Osaka->Kyoto->Tokyo)
3. **Use IC cards** (Suica/ICOCA) for all local Japan transit -- small fare discounts vs buying individual tickets
4. **Eat at convenience stores** in Japan -- 7-Eleven, Lawson, FamilyMart have surprisingly excellent and cheap meals ($3-5)
5. **Use IC cards** (Suica/ICOCA) everywhere in Japan -- small fare discounts vs cash tickets
6. **Free attractions first** -- The Bund, Fushimi Inari, Bamboo Grove, Senso-ji, Meiji Shrine, Tiananmen are all free
7. **Lunch sets in Japan** -- many restaurants offer lunch kaiseki/sets at 50-70% of dinner prices
8. **Tax-free shopping in Japan** -- spend 5,000+ yen at participating stores, show passport, save 10%
9. **Depachika discounts** -- department store basement food halls discount items 30-50% before closing
10. **Street food meals in China** -- jianbing ($1-2), noodles ($2-4), and dumplings ($3-5) are incredibly cheap and delicious
