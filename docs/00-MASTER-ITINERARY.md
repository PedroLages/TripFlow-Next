## Key Pre-Trip Preparation Checklist

### Apps to Download (Before Departure)
- [ ] **Alipay** -- payments in China (link international card + verify passport)
- [ ] **WeChat** -- messaging & payments in China
- [ ] **Didi** -- ride-hailing in China
- [ ] **Gaode Maps (Amap)** -- navigation in China (Google Maps blocked)
- [ ] **VPN app** (ExpressVPN or Astrill) -- install before entering China!
- [ ] **Welcome Suica Mobile** -- IC card for Japan transit (iPhone)
- [ ] **SmartEX** -- book Shinkansen tickets
- [ ] **Fuji-Q Highland app** -- wait times + ride info
- [ ] **USJ app** -- wait times + timed entry
- [ ] **Pleco** -- Chinese-English dictionary
- [ ] **Google Translate** -- download offline Japanese + Chinese packs

### Documents & Bookings
- [ ] Passport valid 6+ months beyond Sep 18
- [ ] China visa or verify 240-hour transit-without-visa eligibility
- [ ] Japan visa (check if needed for your nationality)
- [ ] Travel insurance
- [ ] International driving permit (if planning to drive)
- [ ] eSIM for China (with VPN) -- buy before departure
- [ ] eSIM for Japan -- buy before departure
- [ ] Physical SIM or Octopus card plans for Hong Kong

### Time-Sensitive Bookings
- [ ] **Forbidden City tickets** -- book exactly 7 days before visit at 8PM Beijing time
- [ ] **Fuji-Q Highland Q-Pack** -- book highway bus + 1-Day Free Pass combo online
- [ ] **teamLab Borderless** -- book online in advance (sells out)
- [ ] **USJ Studio Pass + Express Pass** -- buy well in advance online
- [ ] **Hakone Romancecar** -- reserve seats via Odakyu website
- [ ] **All flights** -- book 6-8 weeks ahead for best prices
