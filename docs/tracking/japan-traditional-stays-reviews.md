# Japan Traditional Stays - Star Review System

## Scoring Criteria (1-10 points each)

| Category | What It Measures |
|----------|------------------|
| **Cultural Experience** | How traditional/authentic the stay is |
| **Value** | Worth the money compared to alternatives |
| **Location** | Convenience for sightseeing |
| **Comfort** | Room size, amenities, sleep quality |
| **Food** | Kaiseki dinner & breakfast quality |
| **Uniqueness** | How special/memorable the experience |

**Max Score:** 70 points | **★★★★★** = 50+ | **★★★★☆** = 45-49 | **★★★☆☆** = 40-44

## 💡 Recommendations by Budget & Traveler Type

### For Luxury Seekers (€400+/night)
1. **Hoshinoya Kyoto** - Ultimate luxury ryokan experience
2. **Gion Hatanaka** - Best cultural immersion + luxury

### For Value-Conscious (€150-300/night)
1. **Ryokan Sakanoue** - Authentic ryokan at good price
2. **Kyoto Machiya** - Entire house to yourselves

### For First-Time Ryokan Experience
1. **Ryokan Sakanoue** - Less intimidating, affordable
2. **Yachiyo** - Good value, near temples

### For Cultural Immersion
1. **Gion Hatanaka** - Stay in geisha district
2. **Koyasan Temple Stay** - Sleep in Buddhist temple

### For Couples
1. **Kyoto Ryokan Kinoe** - Intimate, romantic atmosphere
2. **Seikoro** - Excellent kaiseki, romantic
