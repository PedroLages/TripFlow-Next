## Part 2: Suzhou Classical Gardens Route

### Route: Suzhou Station → Humble Administrator's Garden → Liu Garden → Pingjiang Road

**Distance:** ~2.5 km walking | **Time:** 4-5 hours

| Stop | What to See | Time Needed |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Suzhou Railway Station** | Store bags in lockers (3-5 RMB) | 10 min |
| **Taxi/Metro to gardens** | Metro Line 4 to Beisita (北寺塔) or taxi (~15 RMB) | 15 min |
| **Humble Administrator's Garden** (Zhuozheng Yuan) | Largest garden, UNESCO, lotus pond | 90 min |
| Walk to Liu Garden | Through Gusu District streets | 10 min |
| **Liu Garden** (Liu Yuan) | Exquisite smaller garden, rockery | 45 min |
| **Lunch** | Song Shu Gui Noodle Shop | 30 min |
| **Pingjiang Road** | Historic canal street, cafes, shops | 60 min |

### Detailed Walk Instructions

**1. Suzhou Station to Gardens**
- **Option A (Metro):** Take Metro Line 4 from **Suzhou Railway Station** to **Beisita Station** (北寺塔), Exit 4. Walk ~8 minutes to Humble Administrator's Garden.
- **Option B (Taxi):** Taxi from station to garden entrance (~15 RMB, 10 minutes). Recommended if carrying bags.

**2. Humble Administrator's Garden (Zhuozheng Yuan)**
- **Entrance:** 178 Northeast Street (东北街178号)
- **Highlights:** Central pond, pavilions, lotus flowers, zigzag bridges
- **Route:** Enter main gate → Central pond area → Western section → Eastern garden → Museum
- **Photo spots:** Pavilion across from pond, lotus flowers, moon gate reflections
- **Tip:** Rent audio guide (20 RMB) or download English app commentary

**3. Walk to Liu Garden**
- Exit Humble Administrator's Garden, turn right on Northeast Street
- Walk ~400 meters (5 minutes) to Liu Garden entrance
- Pass traditional shops and snack vendors along the way

**4. Liu Garden (Liu Yuan)**
- **Entrance:** 338 Liuyuan Road (留园路338号)
- **Highlights:** Four Seasons rockery, circular moon gates, intimate courtyards
- **Route:** Central hall → Eastern rockery → Western garden → Northern sections
- **Photo spots:** Through moon gates, rockery peaks, reflected architecture

**5. Lunch: Song Shu Gui Noodle Shop**
- **Address:** 101 Fenghuang Street (凤凰街101号) - ~10 min walk from Liu Garden
- **Specialty:** Thin, delicate noodles in rich broth
- **Must-try:** Squirrel-shaped fish, seasonal toppings
- **Price:** 30-50 RMB per person
- **Alternative:** Tongdexing for traditional dim sum

**6. Pingjiang Road Stroll**
- **Start:** Walk from lunch area to Pingjiang Road (5-10 minutes)
- **Route:** Start from **Pingjiang Road Station** end, walk toward **Gusu Gate**
- **Length:** ~1.6 km of canal-side street
- **Highlights:** Stone bridges, traditional houses, tea houses, cafes
- **Photo spots:** Bridge reflections, canal views, lantern displays
- **Shopping:** Silk products, traditional crafts, souvenirs
- **Time:** Late afternoon is ideal for golden light

## Part 4: Zhouzhuang Water Town Route

### Route: Zhouzhuang Entrance → Double Bridges → Shen Hall → Canal Boat Ride

**Distance:** ~1.5 km walking | **Time:** 2-3 hours

| Stop | What to See | Time Needed |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Zhouzhuang Entrance** | Buy ticket (100 RMB), get map | 10 min |
| **Double Bridges** | Shide + Yongan bridges, iconic view | 20 min |
| **Shen Hall** | Wealthy merchant's mansion | 30 min |
| **Zhang Hall** | Ming dynasty residence over water | 20 min |
| **Canal-side stroll** | Traditional architecture, shops | 60 min |
| **Boat ride** | Canal perspective, sunset | 20 min |

### Detailed Walk Instructions

**1. Zhouzhuang Entrance**
- **Ticket:** 100 RMB (includes most attractions)
- **Tip:** Visit late afternoon for fewer crowds + sunset atmosphere
- **Avoid:** Weekends if possible (extremely crowded)

**2. Double Bridges (Shide Bridge + Yongan Bridge)**
- **What:** One bridge square, one round - Yin-Yang symbol
- **Photo spot:** Classic shot from canal level looking up at bridges
- **Best light:** Late afternoon sun creates beautiful reflections

**3. Shen Hall (Shen Ting)**
- **What:** Grand mansion of wealthy Shen family (Qing Dynasty)
- **Highlights:** 100+ rooms, boat dock, private canal access
- **Time:** 30 minutes to explore main rooms

**4. Zhang Hall (Zhang Ting)**
- **What:** Ming dynasty residence built over water
- **Highlights:** Water gate, traditional architecture, canal views

**5. Canal-Side Stroll**
- **Route:** Follow main canals, explore narrow lanes
- **Shopping:** Local crafts, silk embroidery, snacks
- **Photo ops:** Reflections in water, stone bridges, traditional houses

**6. Canal Boat Ride**
- **Cost:** 180-200 RMB per boat (seats 4-6, share with others)
- **Duration:** ~20 minutes
- **Route:** Main canals, under stone bridges
- **Best time:** Sunset for golden light on ancient houses
- **Tip:** Share boat to split cost

## Practical Tips

### Weather Planning (Late August)
- **Temperature:** 25-32°C / 77-90°F
- **Conditions:** Hot, humid, possible afternoon thunderstorms
- **What to bring:** Umbrella, sunscreen, comfortable walking shoes, portable fan
- **Best time:** Start early to avoid midday heat

### Payment & Connectivity
- **Alipay/WeChat Pay:** Essential for most vendors
- **Cash:** Bring 200-300 RMB as backup
- **Internet:** Shanghai eSIM works in Suzhou/Zhouzhuang
- **VPN:** Required for Google, Instagram, etc.

### Timing Strategy
- **Early start:** 7:30 AM train from Shanghai
- **Gardens:** Morning (cooler, fewer crowds)
- **Lunch:** 12:30 PM in Suzhou
- **Pingjiang Road:** 1:30 PM (after lunch rush)
- **Zhouzhuang:** 3:30-6:30 PM (late afternoon atmosphere)
- **Return:** Depart Zhouzhuang by 6:30 PM

## Alternative: If Running Short on Time

**Skip Zhouzhuang** and extend Suzhou time:
- Add **Master of Nets Garden** (smaller, UNESCO)
- Visit **Hanshan Temple** (famous poem)
- Explore more of **Gusu District** lanes
- Return to Shanghai earlier, more relaxed

**Benefits:** Lower cost (~150 RMB less), less rushing, more garden appreciation
