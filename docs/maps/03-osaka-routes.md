## Day 1 | Sep 2 -- Dotonbori Food Crawl

### Route: Namba Arrival → Shinsaibashi → Dotonbori

**Distance:** ~2 km walking | **Time:** 3-4 hours

| Stop | What to See | Time Needed |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **KIX Airport** | Land, immigration, baggage | - |
| **Nankai Rapi:t to Namba** | 34 min to Namba Station | 40 min |
| **Check-in** | Hotel in Namba/Dotonbori area | - |
| **Shinsaibashi Shopping Arcade** | 600m covered shopping street | 45-60 min |
| **Dotonbori evening** | Neon canal district, street food | 2-3 hours |

**Walking route details:**

1. **From KIX:** Take the **Nankai Rapi:t** (34 min) to **Namba Station**. This is the fastest and most direct route to the Dotonbori area.

2. **Shinsaibashi Arcade:** From Namba Station, walk to **Shinsaibashi-suji Shopping Street** -- a 600-meter covered arcade filled with boutiques, chain stores, cafes, and Don Quijote. Great for afternoon shopping and AC relief from September heat.

3. **Dotonbori:** Shinsaibashi leads naturally into **Dotonbori** at the south end. Key spots:
   - **Glico Running Man** sign -- classic photo op at Ebisu Bridge
   - **Canal walk** -- best views at night when neon lights reflect off the water
   - **Takoyaki at Kukuru** -- signature white wine pour over takoyaki
   - **Okonomiyaki at Mizuno or Chibo** -- savory pancakes

**Food crawl strategy:**
- Start with takoyaki (Kukuru or Creo-Ru)
- Move to okonomiyaki (Mizuno has a line but worth it)
- Try gyoza from street stalls
- End with dessert (Rikuro's cheesecake or 551 Horai pork buns)

**Pro tip:** Dotonbori comes alive after 6:00 PM. Plan to be there during golden hour and into the evening for the full neon experience.

## Day 2 | Sep 3 -- Universal Studios Japan

### Route: Full Day at USJ

**Distance:** ~5 km walking in park | **Time:** 8-10 hours

| Stop | What to See | Time Needed |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Depart Namba** | JR to Universal City Station | 30 min |
| **Park opening** | Arrive 30-45 min before gates open | - |
| **Super Nintendo World** | Mario Kart, Yoshi's Adventures | 60-90 min |
| **The Wizarding World of Harry Potter** | Hogwarts Castle, Forbidden Journey | 60-90 min |
| **Lunch** | Inside park or nearby | 45 min |
| **Hollywood Dream** | Roller coaster with forward/backward options | 30 min |
| **The Flying Dinosaur** | Intense flying coaster | 30 min |
| **Return to Namba** | JR from Universal City | 30 min |

**Getting to USJ:**
- From **Namba Station**, take JR to **Nishikujo**, then transfer to JR Yumesaki Line to **Universal City Station** (~30 min total)
- Alternatively, take Osaka Metro to **Bentencho**, transfer to JR Loop Line to **Nishikujo**, then JR Yumesaki Line

**Pro tips:**
- **Arrive 30-45 minutes before opening** -- gates often open early
- **Express Pass 7** is highly recommended for first-timers (~$75-150 USD on top of Studio Pass)
- **Download USJ app** for real-time wait times
- **Without Express Pass:** Rush to Super Nintendo World first thing, grab timed entry ticket immediately via app

**Ride priority order:**
1. Mario Kart (Super Nintendo World) -- lines get longest
2. Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey
3. Hollywood Dream (try backward-facing for extra thrill)
4. The Flying Dinosaur -- one of the world's longest flying coasters

## Day 3 | Sep 4 -- Osaka Highlights

### Route: Kuromon Market → Osaka Castle → Shinsekai → Dotonbori

**Distance:** ~6 km walking | **Time:** Full day

**Morning: Kuromon Market**

| Stop | What to See | Time Needed |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Nippombashi Station** | Metro from Namba | 10 min |
| **Kuromon Market** | "Osaka's Kitchen," 150 shops | 60-90 min |
| **Seafood breakfast** | Grilled crab legs, oysters, sushi | 30-45 min |

**Pro tip:** Arrive before 10:00 AM to experience Kuromon like locals do. Tour groups arrive after 11 AM. Many stalls are cash-only.

**Late Morning: Osaka Castle**

| Stop | What to See | Time Needed |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Osakajokoen Station** | JR Loop Line from Namba | 15 min |
| **Osaka Castle Park** | Extensive grounds, moat | 30 min |
| **Castle Main Tower** | Museum, observation deck (Y600) | 45-60 min |

**Walking route:** From the station, it's a 15-minute walk through the park to the castle. The park is beautiful in the morning and offers great photo opportunities with the castle exterior.

**Afternoon: Shinsekai**

| Stop | What to See | Time Needed |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Shin-Imamiya Station** | JR or Metro from castle area | 20 min |
| **Shinsekai district** | Retro neighborhood, Tsutenkaku Tower | 45-60 min |
| **Kushikatsu lunch** | Daruma (most famous) or Yaekatsu (local favorite) | 45-60 min |
| **Tsutenkaku Tower** | Optional observation deck (Y900) | 20 min |

**Shinsekai highlights:**
- Retro Showa-era atmosphere
- Kushikatsu on every corner -- deep-fried skewers
- Tsutenkaku Tower (neo-classic tower from 1956)
- More local/gritty vibe than Dotonbori

**Important kushikatsu rule:** NEVER double-dip in the communal sauce. One dip only!

**Evening: Back to Dotonbori**

| Stop | What to See | Time Needed |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Return to Namba** | Metro or JR | 15-20 min |
| **Final Osaka dinner** | Ajinoya (okonomiyaki, Michelin Bib Gourmand) | 45-60 min |
| **Late snacks** | 551 Horai pork buns, Rikuro's cheesecake | 30 min |

**Ajinoya:** Located near Hozenji Yokocho in Namba. Walk-ins OK on weekdays before 6:00 PM. Their okonomiyaki is legendary.

## Day 4 | Sep 5 -- Departure for Kyoto

### Route: Osaka → Kyoto (Quick Transit)

| Stop | What to See | Time Needed |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Check out** | Osaka hotel | - |
| **JR Osaka Station** | From Namba (Metro to Umeda) | 15 min |
| **JR Special Rapid to Kyoto** | 30 min, Y580 (~$4 USD) | 30 min |
| **Arrive Kyoto Station** | Check-in to Kyoto hotel | - |

**Pro tip:** The JR Special Rapid from Osaka Station is the best value option. No reservation needed -- just tap your IC card and board. Trains run frequently (~every 15-20 minutes).

## Weather Considerations (Early September)

- **Temperature:** Highs 30-32°C (86-90°F), lows 24-26°C (75-79°F)
- **Humidity:** High at start of September (~73%)
- **Rain:** ~14 rainy days in September; typhoon season active

**Strategy:**
- Outdoor sights in morning (Osaka Castle park, Kuromon before crowds)
- Indoor alternatives: USJ, shopping malls (Shinsaibashi), department stores
- Carry compact umbrella or light rain jacket

## Day Trip Option: Nara (If Time Permits)

If arriving in Osaka earlier than planned, Nara makes a great day trip:

| Route | Time | Cost |
|-------|------|------|
| **Kintetsu from Namba** | ~40 min to Kintetsu Nara | Y760 (~$5) one-way |
| **JR from Osaka** | ~50 min to JR Nara | Y720 (~$5) one-way |

**Nara highlights:** Nara Park (deer!), Todai-ji (Great Buddha), Kasuga Taisha shrine. Budget 5-6 hours for a comfortable day trip.
